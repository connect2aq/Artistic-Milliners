Project Vision

Transform Artistic Milliners' opportunity-to-order process with a unified Salesforce platform that provides real-time visibility, streamlines collaboration between Sales, PD, and R&D teams, and delivers exceptional customer experience through transparent sample development tracking.

üìå Project Overview

Project Name: Salesforce Opportunity-to-Order Lifecycle Implementation

Business Unit: Sales, Product Development (PD), Research & Development (R&D)

Implementation Scope: End-to-end customer opportunity management from initial discovery through sample development, approval, commercial proposal, and order placement.

Primary Objective: Replace fragmented manual processes and disparate systems with a unified Salesforce platform that provides complete visibility and automation across the entire opportunity-to-order lifecycle.

‚ùå Current Business Challenges

Artistic Milliners currently faces several critical challenges in the opportunity-to-order process:

  • Lack of Visibility: Customers cannot track sample development status in real-time, leading to frequent status inquiry calls and reduced satisfaction
  • Manual Handoffs: Multiple handoffs between Sales, PD, and R&D teams rely on emails and spreadsheets, causing delays and miscommunication
  • R&D Cost Uncertainty: R&D costs and timelines are not transparent upfront, leading to customer surprises and deal friction
  • Fragmented Sample Tracking: Sample iterations, revisions, and approvals tracked across multiple systems with no single source of truth
  • Siloed Team Operations: Sales and PD teams operate independently without shared visibility into opportunity status
  • Slow Quote Generation: Manual costing calculations and proposal creation delay deal closure
  • Lost Opportunities: Slow response times and lack of transparency result in lost deals to faster competitors

‚úÖ Proposed Solution

Implement a comprehensive Salesforce solution covering 9 core objects and 32 automated stages:

  • Unified Opportunity Management: Single platform tracking opportunities from discovery to order
  • Transparent Sample Development: Complete visibility into sample iterations, R&D requests, and approval status
  • R&D Request Workflow: Structured 6-stage R&D approval process with customer review and cost transparency
  • Automated Sample Issuance: SAR, SFR, and SYR tracking for physical sample dispatch and customer feedback
  • Commercial Proposal Automation: Streamlined quote generation based on approved samples and final costing
  • ERP Integration: Seamless handoff to ERP for order fulfillment and production planning
  • Customer Portal Integration: Real-time visibility for customers to track their opportunities and samples

The success of this implementation will be measured by:
