Investment Overview

This Salesforce implementation will transform Artistic Milliners' opportunity-to-order lifecycle, delivering measurable improvements in cycle time, customer satisfaction, and win rates while providing complete transparency across Sales, PD, and R&D teams.

Current State vs. Future State

‚ùå Current State - Pain Points
Manual Handoffs
Sales, PD, and R&D teams rely on emails, spreadsheets, and phone calls to communicate opportunity status, leading to delays and miscommunication.
No Customer Visibility
Customers cannot track their sample development status in real-time, resulting in frequent inquiry calls and reduced satisfaction.
R&D Cost Surprises
R&D costs and timelines are not transparent upfront, causing customer surprises and friction during deal closure.
Fragmented Sample Tracking
Sample iterations, revisions, and approvals tracked across multiple systems with no single source of truth.
Slow Quote Generation
Manual costing calculations and proposal creation delay commercial proposals by several days.
Lost Competitive Advantage
Slow response times and lack of transparency result in lost deals to faster, more transparent competitors.
‚úÖ Future State - Salesforce Solution
Automated Workflows
32 automated stages seamlessly transition opportunities from discovery through R&D, sample development, and order placement.
Customer Portal
Self-service portal provides real-time visibility into opportunity status, sample development progress, and timelines.
Transparent R&D Costing
Upfront R&D cost estimates with customer approval workflow prevent surprises and build trust.
Unified Sample Management
Single platform tracks all sample iterations (SAR, SFR, SYR), R&D requests, and approvals with complete audit trail.
Automated Quote Generation
Commercial proposals generated automatically from approved samples and costing data, delivered in hours not days.
Competitive Differentiation
Industry-leading transparency and speed create competitive advantage and increase win rates.

Risk Analysis & Mitigation

MEDIUM

User Adoption Resistance

Sales, PD, and R&D teams may resist transitioning from familiar manual processes to new Salesforce platform.

Comprehensive training program, phased rollout, super-user champions, executive sponsorship
MEDIUM

ERP Integration Complexity

Integration between Salesforce and existing ERP system may encounter technical challenges or data mapping issues.

Dedicated integration specialist, proof-of-concept testing, fallback manual handoff procedures
LOW

Data Migration Quality

Historical opportunity and sample data may have quality issues during migration from legacy systems.

Data cleansing phase, validation rules, go-live with clean slate for new opportunities
LOW

Scope Creep

Additional feature requests during implementation may delay timeline and increase budget.

Clear scope definition, change control process, phased feature releases, backlog prioritization
MEDIUM

Customer Portal Adoption

Customers may not actively use the self-service portal for tracking opportunities and samples.

Customer onboarding program, portal walkthrough videos, email notifications with portal links
