Governance Model

This Salesforce implementation requires close collaboration between business stakeholders (Sales, PD, R&D leadership), IT teams, Salesforce developers, and external implementation partners. Clear roles and decision-making authority ensure efficient project execution and timely issue resolution.

üë• Engagement Model

Steering Committee Meetings: Monthly (Executive Sponsor, Sales/PD/R&D Directors, Project Manager)

Core Team Meetings: Weekly (Project Manager, Business Analyst, SF Developer, Integration Specialist)

Business Stakeholder Reviews: Bi-weekly (Sales, PD, R&D leads + Core Team for requirements validation)

UAT Sessions: Daily during UAT phase (Business users + QA team)

Training Sessions: As scheduled during rollout (Change Management Lead + end users)

Success Depends on Collaboration

This implementation requires active participation from Sales, PD, and R&D stakeholders throughout requirements gathering, design reviews, UAT, and training. Clear roles, decision authority, and regular communication ensure the project delivers a solution that meets business needs and drives user adoption.
