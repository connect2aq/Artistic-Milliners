Project Mission

Implement a unified Salesforce platform that streamlines the entire opportunity-to-order lifecycle, providing real-time visibility, automated workflows, and exceptional customer experience across Sales, Product Development, and R&D teams.

Project Objectives

01
Unified Platform
Replace fragmented manual processes with a single Salesforce platform managing opportunities from discovery to order.
02
Complete Transparency
Provide real-time visibility into sample development status, R&D progress, and timelines for customers and internal teams.
03
Automated Workflows
Implement 32 automated stages that eliminate manual handoffs and ensure consistent process execution.
04
Customer Portal
Deploy self-service portal enabling customers to track opportunities, samples, and approvals independently.
05
ERP Integration
Seamlessly integrate Salesforce with existing ERP for order handoff and production planning.
06
Faster Deal Closure
Reduce opportunity-to-order cycle time by 40% through automation and eliminating process bottlenecks.

üîë Key Assumptions & Dependencies

ERP API Availability
Existing ERP system has documented APIs or integration endpoints available for order handoff and master data synchronization.
Business Process Standardization
Sales, PD, and R&D teams will align on standardized processes and workflows as defined in the FSD, replacing ad-hoc variations.
Dedicated Project Team
Business analysts, SMEs, and testers will be available part-time throughout the implementation for requirements validation and UAT.
Customer Portal Access
Customers are willing to create accounts and use the Experience Cloud portal for tracking opportunities and samples.
Data Migration Scope
Only active/recent opportunities will be migrated. Historical closed opportunities will remain in legacy systems for reference.
Executive Sponsorship
Executive leadership will actively champion the change, communicate vision, and hold teams accountable for adoption.
Salesforce Licensing
Appropriate Salesforce licenses (Sales Cloud, Service Cloud for portal) will be procured in time for implementation.
Network & Infrastructure
Network connectivity, VPN access, and security policies support Salesforce cloud access for internal and external users.

Scope Summary

This Phase 1 implementation focuses exclusively on the opportunity-to-order lifecycle, delivering complete end-to-end functionality for Sales, PD, and R&D teams. Future phases will expand into marketing automation, service & support, advanced analytics, and additional ERP integrations based on lessons learned and business priorities.
