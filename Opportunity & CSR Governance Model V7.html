This model explains how a customer requirement moves from first discussion to final Purchase Order ‚Äî covering sales qualification, product development, sampling, approvals, and commercial negotiation ‚Äî with clear ownership and visibility at every step.

From Stage To Stage When This Happens

The Customer Sample Request (CSR) formally controls when execution is allowed to begin. Until CSR enters In Execution, no sampling or development work starts.

R&D Development ‚Äì When New Technical Work Is Required

This is a simplified, customer-facing view of our R&D process. Internally, R&D follows a more detailed, controlled workflow to ensure feasibility, quality, and cost discipline.

R&D is initiated only when a requirement cannot be fulfilled using existing designs or standard development. This ensures innovation is controlled, feasible, and commercially viable.
